Problem

Arsenic Poisoning due to contaminated groundwater

The contamination in drinking water or ground water (includes, As, Fe, Pathogens, Nitrate, Lead) is a serious health concern. Being causes many health problems such as: -

  • Vascular diseases
  • Cancers and Acute lesions,
  • Keratosis and Diarrhea
  • And most of the water borne diseases

Water contamination levels should be distinctly managed in groundwater wells; therefore, quality monitoring is a primary target in utilities and environmental management.

Detecting heavy metals and trace elements with the consumption of chemical and accessories in water is difficult. Sites driven to, analytical laboratories introduce delays, and field kits are difficult to use or lack data integrity.

Features

Advance & compact system for ground and surface water contaminants monitors simultaneous quantitative assessment of: -

Heavy metal and Trace elements

Applying novel methods includes Magnetostatic & Quantum Magnetic Behavioral of Molecules, to asses the level of concentration in ppb at running water.

Physical parameters,

Implemented the Commercials Digital Sensors and circuitry to quantify the relevant factors of pH, ORP, TDS, Ec.

Bacteriological

Embedded System, based sensor to provide the MPN/ml value of Pathogens presence.
